Call for Manuscripts

The Dialogue solicits writing from Fellows of the San Diego Area Writing Project. Our aim is to provide a vital network for the dissemination of information and ideas.

The Dialogue would like to receive your work or the work of your students. Please submit via email.

SDAWP Project Notes

Our Project Notes column acknowledges the achievements of San Diego Area Writing Project Fellows.

Which Writing Project member:

  • Has received an award, grant or special recognition?
  • Has had a story, essay, poem, book, or professional writing published?
  • Has presented work at a local or national conference?
  • Has created a writing, study, or reading group?

Please let us know what you know. Send your acknowledgments to The Dialogue via the email address below in the Contact drawer.

Letters to the Editor

We encourage you to agree or disagree with a viewpoint expressed or to suggest a theme or topic for a future issue. Send your letters or suggestions to the SDAWP Office or to the editors via email.

Contact

Email manuscript submissions, suggestions, letters to the editor, and Project Notes to:

  • San Diego Area Writing Project (sdawp@ucsd.edu)
